SMS Program Description

Ambio Life Sciences may send one-time transactional SMS messages to existing patients regarding treatment programs, appointment reminders, scheduling updates, prescription or medication instructions, post-treatment follow-up, and other direct healthcare communications.

SMS messages are sent only to patients who have provided their phone number and consented to receive text messages related to their care.

Ambio Life Sciences does not send promotional or marketing SMS messages through this campaign.

How Patients Opt In

Patients may opt in through one or more of the following consent paths:

  1. Website or online intake: Patients provide their mobile phone number during online registration, intake, account creation, or while updating their patient record.
  2. Consent disclosure: Patients are shown SMS consent language before submitting their information.
  3. Affirmative consent: Patients check a consent checkbox or sign a consent form confirming that they agree to receive SMS messages related to treatment, appointments, scheduling, medication or prescription instructions, post-treatment follow-up, and care coordination.
  4. CRM record update: Patient consent status is stored in Ambio Life Sciences’ patient management process and Microsoft Dynamics CRM.
  5. Message delivery: SMS messages are sent only after consent has been collected and recorded.

Required SMS Disclosures

  • Program name: Ambio Life Sciences patient communications.
  • Purpose: Appointment reminders, scheduling updates, treatment-related communications, medication or prescription instructions, post-treatment follow-up, and care coordination.
  • Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on the patient’s care, appointment schedule, and treatment-related updates.
  • Fees: Message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to opt out of future SMS messages.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance or contact info@ambio.life.
  • Privacy: Mobile numbers are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on each patient’s appointments, treatment program, scheduling updates, medication or prescription instructions, and care-related communications. Patients may receive messages only when there is a relevant transactional update or reminder.

Opt-Out Instructions

Patients may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message received from Ambio Life Sciences.

After opting out, patients may no longer receive SMS appointment reminders, care updates, or scheduling-related text messages unless they opt back in through an approved consent process.

Help Instructions

Patients may reply HELP to any SMS message for assistance. Patients may also contact Ambio Life Sciences directly at info@ambio.life.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on the patient’s mobile carrier and plan.

Privacy and Mobile Number Sharing

Ambio Life Sciences respects patient privacy. Mobile phone numbers collected for SMS communications are used only for patient-related transactional communications.

Ambio Life Sciences does not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.
